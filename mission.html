Our Mission

Our mission is to empower the discovery of exoplanets through cutting-edge AI technologies, enabling faster and more accurate identification of worlds beyond our solar system.

By harnessing rich datasets from Kepler and TESS telescopes, combined with advanced machine learning, we streamline the search for habitable planets and contribute to humanity’s understanding of the cosmos.

We commit to openness, collaboration, and education, providing tools that inspire both researchers and enthusiasts worldwide to explore the universe.
